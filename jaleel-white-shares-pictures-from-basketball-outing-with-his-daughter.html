    18 May 2024, 00:00

    Jaleel White is determined to end the year with a bang. The celebrity father recently shared pictures from his recent trip to the basketball arena with his daughter, Samaya White.

    “Another year around the sun, goin’ out with a bang,” Jaleel told his social media fans. “#GirlDad,” the celebrity father hashtagged before informing his supporters that seeing a live basketball game at the Chase Center in San Francisco was a goal on his bucket list.

    “Great home win, best basketball arena hands down,” the celebrity father said of the evening of fun with his daughter. “Thanks for the hospitality fellas @chrismullin2017 @fezzyfel @djdsharp,” White shared.

    Samaya crossed a significant milestone earlier this year. The youngster turned thirteen years old in September and received the ultimate present of concert tickets to see The Weekend at SoFi Stadium. “All she really wanted for her 13th birthday this year was to see @theweeknd live in concert,” Jaleel told his social media followers. “Your wish is granted,” the celebrity dad hashtagged.

    White is focused on making his daughter happy. It was just last year that the actor took to social media to share what he has found to be the secret to his peace.

    “My life criteria for success has (sic) simplified tremendously over the years,” Jaleel told his supporters. “As long as the amazing women in my life are healthy happy and smiling on most days, that is a direct reflection of just how much I am winning,” White revealed. “I am surrounded by daily laughter and love,” the actor shared with his social media fans. “Family over everything.”

    Many fans praised Jaleel White for taking his daughter to see The Weekend in concert. “Guaranteed she will never forget this,” one Instagram follower declared. “This is everything,” another supporter said.
