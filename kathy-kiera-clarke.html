    Kathy Kiera Clarke Career

    Clarke’s first television role was as Bernadette Brennan in Head over Heels in 1993. Clarke has appeared in several comedic and dramatic roles over the years. In 1995, she appeared in an episode of Chandler & Co as Poppy Farquhar. She also played Rosemary in the 1996 film Eskimo Day. She played Rosie in the film The Most Fertile Man in Ireland in 2000.

    In 2003, she acted in the film Solid Air. She has played many roles over the years, including Marlene Riley in The Ice Cream Girls (2013), Varvara in Bitter Harvest (2017), and Aunt Sarah in the Channel 4 sitcom Derry Girls (2018). In the year 2020, actress Clarke took on the character of Sybil Stamfordis for the BBC One mini-series The Pale Horse. In 2021, Clarke played Claire Keenan in Bloodlands.

    Some of her more notable parts include Una in Pulling Moves, Elizabeth Gibson in Omagh, Jenifer Gaghan in two episodes of Silent Witness, Sinead in four episodes of Proof, Agnesin Small Engine Repair and Emma Cherrybomb.
