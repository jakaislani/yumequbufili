    Christina Aguilera’s kids: heart-melting cuties

    One might know Christina Aguilera for her persona when it comes to Pop, R&B and soul music. More than these, there is an album named Christina Aguilera.

    Coincidentally, it was the debut album that contributed greatly to her rise in prominence.

    Aguilera was nicknamed the “Princess of Pop” and she proved the name by earning several Grammys and top placements on music charts.

    SUBSCRIBE!

     

    Christina Aguilera husband

    Jordan Bratman (ex-spouse)

    Duration of Marriage: November 19, 2005 – 2011

    Date of Birth: June 4, 1977

    Zodiac Sign: Gemini

    Christina Maria got married to Jordan Bratman on November 19, 2005 in Napa County, California. Her marriage to the hot music marketing executive ended after 6 years as they divorced in 2011. Before the divorce, she had a son with him.

    Matthew Rutler (fiance)

    Duration of Relations: since 2010 till present

    Date of Birth: April 14, 1985

    Zodiac Sign: Aries

    In 2010, Christina had a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. That same year, she began dating Matthew Rutler and got engaged to him in 2014. Aguilera has a little daughter with Rutler.

    Etta James’s biggest fan Christina is just as passionate about her kids as she is with music. She says it’s awesome to have kids and that they are the best things in this world. Aguilera also refers to them as the centre of her universe.

    Who are these lovelies that she birthed?


    Christina Aguilera children

    Max Liron Bratman (son, born to Christina Aguilera and Jordan Bratman)

    Date of Birth: January 12, 2008

    Zodiac Sign: Capricorn

    With the whole world being a fan of ‘The Voice’, Aguilera’s son Max is not a fan. He’s never on board in the production of the show and other projects. As long as it is not Ninja Warriors, Max Liron is less concerned. Aguilera is definitely doing a great job as a mum despite her busy career; she juggles between work and motherhood beautifully. Max also gets to have his much needed “mummy time” every now and then.

    Christina says Max might have in mind a career in acrobatics. The little Bratman is so gifted with his body balance and is often seen around the house performing acrobatics like he’s in a circus tent. I can imagine how much energy flows around in their home.

    Summer Rain Rutler (daughter, born to Christina Aguilera and Matthew Rutler)

    Date of Birth: August 16, 2014

    Zodiac Sign: Leo

    Isn’t she just an adorable cutie? She loves her parents, little Max, the number 7, Minnie Mouse, dogs and of course, all things pink! Aguilera and Rutler obviously poured so much of their beauty into this little pumpkin. She has pretty blonde hair and big blue eyes that always attract people around her. She’s not much trouble on set as she is a people person.

    Remember when you were a kid and you always did exactly what your older sibling does? For Summer Rain, she has the tough brother Max for an elder sibling so imagine whose steps she gets to follow. She goes from climbing walls and everything else to wearing Max’s boyish hand-me-downs. Nothing could be cuter than this anyway!

    It’s lovely that Christina has more than a success in musical career going on for her. Her kids are absolutely everything! So much for love, beauty and energy when she’s back home.
