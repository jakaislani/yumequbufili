    Tom Segura Bio and Age

    Born on April 16, 1979 in Cincinnati, Ohio, TomSegura was raised alongside his sister. He is Peruvian, French and Spanish at the same time. It seems his dad gave him some of his fun sides, which he says is a regular contributor to his show. Growing up, Tom lived in places such as Minneapolis, Milwaukee, as well as Vero Beach, Florida. For his education, the Shameful comedian went to Lenoir-Rhyne University. After finishing his studies, he decided to become a comedian. He went on to reveal that his journey into the world of comedy was all about containing all his frustrations.

    Tom had quite an interesting life which was not without its many problems. He almost lost his life at the age of 18 when he took a drug overdose which then put him in a coma. It was his sister who called an ambulance to try to save his life. The only thing that helped him survive was the fact that he was fat.

    Today, Tom Segura directs his own specials on Netflix, including Tom Segura: Shameful and Tom Segura: Mostly Stories. More so, he is also very popular when it comes to podcasts like “The Joe Rogan Experience” and “WTF with Marc Maron”. He also has his “Your Mom’s House” podcast which he runs with his wife, Christina Pazsitzky. The podcast has a lot of very dedicated followers and it was inspired by Rogan who also has a very successful podcast. Rogan asked Segura and his wife to start the BD podcast after realizing they were both in the same situation.

    Segura hasn’t been without his controversies as people wrote to Netflix to censor the comedian’s show Disgraceful on its platform. Different individuals and activists, including the Canadian Down Syndrome Society, have accused him of hate speech after he used the word “retarded” in a joke. Speaking to Change.org, they asked that the jokes are ‘offensive and disrespectful’ and should therefore be taken down. This is the biggest controversy he has entered into.
