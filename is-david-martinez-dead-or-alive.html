    Synopsis of the plot

    A young boy named David Martinez lives in Night City and is enrolled in the Arasaka academy there. David, in contrast to his classmates, is not wealthy and shares a home with his mother, who works only as a primary medic.

    David is a model student who earns only A’s, but he is socially isolated from the other students in his class. With the assistance of his close pal Ripperdoc, he takes shortcuts when it comes to his cyber implants. His mother wants him to put his education first and work his way up the ranks at the Arasaka firm as quickly as possible.

    On a certain day, David and his mother are unfortunate enough to get caught in the crossfire of a drive-by shooting, which leaves his mother in a critical condition. She is sent to a mediocre doctor because it is the only option available to them, but she does not make it through treatment.

    David discovers a Sandevistan implant from Arasaka, which is a military-grade cybernetic device, while searching through her possessions. Recently, he witnessed a soldier going through cyberpsychosis while using it, and an elite Max Tac squad was eventually able to take him down.

He then travels back home with Lucy, where the two of them talk about the goals they had set for themselves. She reveals to him that one of her goals is to travel to the moon, which is a well-known location for vacationers.

They come to an agreement that he can join them and that they will send him on a mission to protect Mr. Tanaka’s limousine’s navigation data. Tanaka is a high-ranking Arasaka executive.

    Lucy, Dorio, Kiwi, Pilar, and Rebecca are some of the members of the crew, which is captained by Maine. They make an effort to carry out the task in a stealthy manner; nevertheless, things go out of hand, and they are forced to improvise. After a drawn-out pursuit, David is finally successful in delivering the limo to its intended destination.

Lucy is David’s roommate, but despite his repeated invitations, she never works with him or his colleagues.
